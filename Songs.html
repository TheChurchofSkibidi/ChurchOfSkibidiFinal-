Oh, Skibidi

Oh, Skibbidi oh Skibbidi let me worship you oh so sigmaly I see dear Skibbidi I see him mewing oh so beautifully in goonland forever my we worship him sigmaly Skibbidi oh I see the bowl when you take me by the hand and guide me through with no demands oh Skibbidi may we see you are the gooning goat with the gyatt of worship and the a bowl of grace.

Skibidi's Bowl

Skibidi, Skibidi so deep in the bowl, so alpha, never fantom taxing those who are mogged, so tremendously proud of the sigmas who take the lead. Oh, do you see him mewing in his bowl, so brilliantly may we see him exquisite in his bowl, never being mogged, let him sing this melody for humanity, let him guide you to power and kindness, and may we let his toilet flushing lead us in to the light.
