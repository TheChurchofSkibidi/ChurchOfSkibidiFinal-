Church Of Skibidi

Done with respect for nature—our mission was a true reflection of Skibidi’s teachings. As we camped under the open sky, we embraced courage in service and honesty in fellowship, deepening our bond with one another and with Him. This experience renewed our purpose, reminding us that faith is lived through action, together in unity.

Kindness reflects Skibidi’s teachings, strengthening souls and bringing light through every act of care.

In Skibidism, baptizing babies and children is considered taboo, as we believe baptism should be a sacred, conscious choice—one that individuals must opt into when they are ready to embrace their faith.

Imbued with Power ● Led with Respect ● Strengthened by Acceptance ● Guided by Courage ● and Defined by Honesty.

Here at The Church of Skibidi, we aim to create a world full of kindness, love, acceptance courage, and honesty. Through the teachings of Skibidi, we create a community of people working to make the world around them a better, happier place. All genders races and sexualities are welcome here, bigotry has no place here. We would love to have you here for our next meet!
